After graduating as interior architect I have worked in several construction sites mainly on mixed used and 5 star hotel projects. Over the past 5 years I have specialized on designing and coordinating every stages of furniture and accessories. For the last two years I have been working on hand built ceramic furnitures using clay as main material, focusing on abstract curves and textures inspired by the nature and biomimicry. My future aim is to use recycled plastic material to produce my designs and be part of a team working on environmental issues.
